About Us

Cursive Text is a project by Vertex Logics LTD dedicated to the Spanish-speaking community. Our goal is to provide simple and free tools
that facilitate text formatting for social networks and messaging platforms like WhatsApp.

Our main tool, Cursive Text allows you to transform your ordinary writing into striking and elegant italic styles. The Cursive
Text team continuously works to improve this tool and develop new ones. Visit the site regularly to discover what's new!
